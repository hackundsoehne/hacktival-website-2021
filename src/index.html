About

What is Hacktival?

We want to motivate as many people as possible to join the hacking community. We want to organize an event where young hackers can work on their own pet projects, while experienced hackers can compete for prizes in ambitious categories.
We want to unite participants from all kinds of backgrounds. We want designers, organizers and artists to join the community of hackers and makers. We want people to pour all their creativity into their project in an inspiring environment.

Smart Assistants – The Future of Customer Experience

CAS

Hi, we’re CAS. We enjoy shaping the future together. Which is why we need open-minded people like you, people who want to be part of something big, people with values, creativity and skills.

The 21st century is taking us to another level technologically. Just think about data analytics, machine learning or artificial intelligence in general. The challenge is to create smart assistants for the customer experience of the future. Build smart assistants that:
· bring together humans and AI Technology
· improve processes, customer interaction, supply chains or
· anything else that comes into your mind.

Feel free to create any kind smart assistant that improves any part of the customer experience. Use predictions, natural language processing, neural networks, etc. that could be useful. Think of new ways to support the customer journey, the sales process, the customer interaction or any kind of business processes. Tackle current and future challenges of customers and businesses and show us what you can do: build an app, a website, an integrated service, … Be creative and design something innovative!

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend?

Anyone!

If you’re reading this, you can attend. This includes people who have never been to a hackathon before. You do not need to be an expert to participate, and the people at the event are super friendly. We have tons of mentors and helpers to support you, so please do come over!

Does this cost money?

Absolutely not! The event is financed entirely by our awesome sponsors.

What size can a team be?

Teams can be up to 4 people. If you are still looking for a team, don’t worry, we will have team building session at the event.

I have never been to a hackathon, what now?

Try it! People are super friendly, and no, you do not have to be an expert to participate. We have tons of mentors and helpers ready to help you, so please come over!

Can I work on my idea before the event starts?

No. Coming up with ideas before the hackathon is fine, but working on them is only allowed as soon as the event starts.
